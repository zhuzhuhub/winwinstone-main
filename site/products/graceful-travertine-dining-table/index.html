Buyer Questions

Common questions before ordering custom stone products.

These are the questions buyers usually ask before moving from reference stage to quote confirmation and production review.

Can I send a reference design?

Yes. You can send photos, sketches, or CAD drawings and we will review the production method.

Can the table be shipped in separate parts?

Yes. Heavy stone tables are often packed with top and base separated for safer shipping.

Do you make coffee tables too?

Yes. Dining tables, coffee tables, side tables, consoles, and plinths can be customized.
